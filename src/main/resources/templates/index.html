Amazon Stock

Investing in Amazon stock has appealed to many investors due to the company's strong market position, extensive product and service offerings, and its ability to disrupt traditional industries. Amazon's core business is its e-commerce platform, which allows individuals and businesses to sell products to customers worldwide. Additionally, Amazon Web Services (AWS) provides cloud computing services to companies, generating substantial revenue for the company

Google Stock

Google's stock, traded under the ticker symbol "GOOGL" on the NASDAQ stock exchange, has historically been a popular investment choice. The company has experienced significant growth and success since its inception. It is known for its dominant search engine, Google Search, as well as its other products and services such as Google Ads, YouTube, Android, Google Cloud, and Google Maps.
