What are Stocks?

Stocks are shares of ownership in a company. When you buy a stock, you become a partial owner of the company and have the potential to profit if the company's value increases.

Stocks are bought and sold on stock exchanges, and their prices can fluctuate based on various factors such as the company's financial performance, market conditions, and investor sentiment.

Investing in stocks can be a way to grow your wealth over time, but it's important to research and understand the risks associated with investing in the stock market.

Why Invest in Stocks?

Investing in stocks offers several potential advantages:

  • Potential for higher returns compared to other investment options
  • Opportunity to participate in the growth of successful companies
  • Ability to diversify your investment portfolio
  • Possibility of earning dividends from profitable companies
  • Liquidity, as stocks can be easily bought and sold on stock exchanges
